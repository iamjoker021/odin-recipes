Rasam Saadam

This is South indian dish to eaten with Rice

This dish helps in better digestion so people eat rice with rasam after their heavy food in mariiages

  • Tamarin
  • Tomato
  • Pepper
  • Masalas...

  2. Fry the Jeera, pepper, Red Chilli in fry pane
  3. Add tomato and sqeexe inside Tamarin water and add masalas and salt
  4. Then mix the tamarin mix water in fry pan with the created jeera fry
  5. Let it cook slowly and turn off once singlr boild happens
